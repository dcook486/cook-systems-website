About

A little about me

I'm David Cook. I have an MBA and experience as a Product Owner in enterprise tech. I started Cook Systems Consulting because I kept seeing small businesses struggle with the same problems that bigger companies have entire teams for — but without the budget or bandwidth to solve them.

I'm not going to pretend I have all the answers. But I'm good at listening, I learn fast, and I genuinely care about getting things right. I'd rather be honest about what I can do than overpromise.

If we work together, you'll get my full attention. I'll work alongside your team, not above them, and I'll make sure anything we build together is something you can actually maintain after I'm gone.
